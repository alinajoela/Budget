**The situation**
We are spending [INSERT: annual digital marketing budget, e.g. €1.2M] on digital marketing across our CHC and RX brand portfolios, and we have no reliable way to connect that spend to e-commerce outcomes. Campaign performance sits with agencies in separate reports. E-commerce data sits in a separate system. No one is currently drawing the line between them.

The cost of leaving this as it is: we continue investing [INSERT: same figure] annually with no way to attribute it to sales. We optimise based on agency reports that measure campaign activity, not commercial results. Every budget decision we make is based on incomplete information.

**What I am proposing**
In my first 90 days I will deliver STADA's first integrated digital performance dashboard — a single view connecting campaign activity across all brands to e-commerce outcomes. It will show, in one place: what we are spending, what it is generating in traffic, and what is converting to sales.

This serves both business units directly. For CHC: connecting consumer campaign spend to online sales of our consumer health brands. For RX: connecting HCP digital engagement to measurable channel outcomes. One dashboard, two lenses.

This is not a reporting exercise. It is the commercial foundation for every digital investment decision we make going forward.

**What this delivers**
- Full visibility into digital marketing ROI — for the first time
- A shared performance language between brand teams, agencies, and leadership
- The baseline from which we set targets and measure e-commerce growth
- A tool brand managers in both CHC and RX can use to make faster, better campaign decisions

**What I need — three specific things**

1. **A communication from [CMO/CCO name] to all brand managers** requesting they share campaign data and agency access with my team. Without this, agencies will not release data. This is a 10-minute action with significant downstream impact.

2. **Budget approval for a 60-day analytics contractor** to build the data infrastructure — estimated €15,000–€25,000 based on comparable builds in similar markets. This is a one-time build cost, not an ongoing headcount commitment. Final figure confirmed in November once I assess existing systems.

3. **Formal authorisation to request campaign reporting data** from our media agencies under existing contracts.

**One consideration to note:** connecting campaign data to e-commerce data across systems involves data flows that will require a light-touch GDPR and data governance review. I will initiate this in parallel and flag anything that affects timeline.
