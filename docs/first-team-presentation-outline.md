# First Team Presentation — STADA Baltics Digital Team
**"Digital at STADA Baltics — where we're going"**
*8 slides, two interaction moments, no intro*

---

## Slide 1: The honest starting point
**Point to make:** Digital here has been running without a clear direction. That changes now.
**Opening question:** "Before I show you where we're going — show of hands. How many of you feel clear on what digital means for your brand or your team right now?"

---

## Slide 2: Three things I'm here to build
**Point to make:** The role has three pillars — equal weight, all connected.
1. E-commerce — growing our online sales channel
2. Digital activation — reaching HCPs and CHC consumers where they are
3. AI integration — making all of the above faster and smarter

---

## Slide 3: E-commerce — the commercial priority
**Point to make:** This is the number the business cares about most. Here is where we are and where we need to get to.
Name the gap. Name the opportunity. Keep it commercial.

---

## Slide 4: Digital activation — HCPs and consumer brands
**Point to make:** Brand managers are running campaigns. We're going to make them connected, measurable, and effective.
One line on CHC, one line on RX/HCP. Equal, not the same.

---

## Slide 5: AI integration — what this actually means here
**Point to make:** AI is not a separate initiative. It is how we do the other two things better.
Two or three concrete examples tied to e-commerce and activation — not abstract AI talk.

---

## Slide 6: I want to hear from you
**Point to make:** You need their reality to build this right.
**Interaction moment:** Two questions to the room:
- "Where is digital currently helping you?"
- "Where is it letting you down?"

---

## Slide 7: How we work together
**Point to make:** What they can expect from you, and what you need from them.
Short. Setting the working relationship, not writing a charter.

---

## Slide 8: One thing. This week.
**Point to make:** Direction is set. Movement starts now.
One concrete action each person leaves with.
