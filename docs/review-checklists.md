# Review Checklists
*Head of Digital & AI, STADA — built during Claude Code mastery course*

---

## 1. Strategic Document Checklist
*For proposals, briefs, and strategy papers*

1. Does the opening sentence make someone care — without any background first?
2. Is there one specific, unambiguous ask — and only one?
3. Does every number in this document have a source or basis — or is it invented?
4. Could this document have been written for any pharma company, or does it contain something specific to STADA?
5. Is the business unit affected (CHC, RX, or both) named explicitly — and are the implications for each addressed?
6. Has the author stated what happens if we do nothing?
7. Does anything in this document require regulatory or compliance consideration — and if so, has it been flagged?
8. Would a skeptical CFO read this and know exactly what it costs and why it's worth it?

---

## 2. AI-Generated Content Checklist
*Before any AI-assisted work leaves your team*

1. Does this contain at least one detail that only someone working at STADA could have written?
2. Does the author take a clear position — or does it hedge?
3. Are any of these phrases present: "in today's rapidly evolving," "it's important to note," "comprehensive approach," "leverage," "synergies"?
4. Does the opening sentence lead with the point — or does it warm up first?
5. Is the length matched to the task — or does it feel like a template was filled in?
6. Read the last paragraph. Does it end with a conclusion — or a generic call to action?

**The one-line test when you're short on time:**
"Does this contain anything only a person who works here could have written — and does it take a clear position?"

---

## When to use which

| Work type | Use |
|---|---|
| Brief or proposal from your team | Strategic Document Checklist |
| Agency proposal or vendor pitch | Strategic Document Checklist |
| Anything produced with AI assistance | AI-Generated Content Checklist |
| AI-assisted strategic document | Both |
